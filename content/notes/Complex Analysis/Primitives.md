Let $f(z) = \sum\_{n=0}^\infty a\_n z^n$ and let it converge for $|z| < R$. We want to find a primitive for $f$. Integrating term by term would give 
$$
F(z) = \sum\_{n=0}^\infty \frac{a\_n}{n+1}z^{n+1}
$$which converges on $|z| < R$.
